About

Business, as a vehicle for change

I believe business is one of the most powerful tools we have for creating real change — and I've spent the last three years proving that to myself, one venture at a time.

My focus sits at the intersection of health & wellness and sustainability — two industries I genuinely care about and think are essential to how we live and build going forward. I'm energised by the early, hands-on part of building something: sourcing the first batch, closing the first deal, finding the first hundred customers.

Across founder roles, founder's-office work, and on-ground business development, I've learned what it actually takes to turn an idea into something real — and I'm looking for my next challenge, with people who want to build the same way.
